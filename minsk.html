Don't Say Beetlejuice in Minsk

When you're in Minsk, you really must visit Beetlejuice bar. Located a little outside the centre, this is worth the walk.

Here you'll find a great range of drinks, indie and rock and roll music and laid-back crowd.

I highly recommend the potato pancakes, a Belarus favourite and massively tasty with extras such as sour cream and bacon. Enjoy!

Address Ulitsa Kalinina 7, Minsk, Belarus.
Opening Hours Sun - Thu: 12pm - 12am
Fri, Sat, Sun: 12pm - 2am
