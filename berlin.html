Swing out at Berlin's Best Hidden Bar

Kreuzberg is pegged as Berlin's party district, with good reason.

Nestled in it's frenetic heart is a bar hidden behind a black door. If you manage to find it and squeeze inside, you'll have to swing, rather than sit.

Old wooden tables are flanked by swings suspended from the ceiling. If you're feeling adventurous, climb up into a wooden box, also hanging by nothing but rope.

Drinks are dirt-cheap, the opening hours and liberal to say the least and the door man is a Liverpool fan. What more could you want!?
